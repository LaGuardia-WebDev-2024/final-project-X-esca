Impressionist Paintings

Impressionism was a 19th-century art movement characterized by visible brush strokes, open composition, and emphasis on ordinary subject matter. The most well known impressionist painters are Van Gogh and Claude Monet, but my favorite is Camille Pissarro.

Cute Cafes

Nothing is more satisfying than finding a cute cafe and spending the afternoon there with your drink of choice. You could bring a novel, sketchbook, computer, or just people watch and enjoy the ambiance. My favorite drinks to get are a london fog or matcha latte.
