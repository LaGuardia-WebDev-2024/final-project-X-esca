~Video Game Recs

Stardew Valley

You've inherited your grandfather's old farm plot in Stardew Valley. Armed with hand-me-down tools and a few coins, you set out to begin your new life! The cute pixel art and amazing soundtrack make Stardew relaxing and super fun to play. This is my favorite video game of all time!

Potion Craft

Potion Craft is an alchemist simulator where you physically interact with your tools and ingredients to brew potions, increase the reputation of your shop and discover new ingredients to create the best concoctions in town! I love the medival art style and the game play!
